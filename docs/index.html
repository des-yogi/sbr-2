Accessibility

What is accessible bathrooms

An accessible bathroom is a space that is designed to accommodate the needs of people with disabilities or reduced mobility. Such bathrooms are equipped with the following elements:

Barrier-free entry

No thresholds or steps, making entry and exit easier and enough space to maneuver a wheelchair or walker

Anti-slip coatings

Use of materials that prevent slipping, especially in wet environments

Supporting elements

Installing grab bars near toilets, showers and bathtubs for added support

Lightweight installations

Toilets and sinks are located at an optimal and comfortable height for sitting or standing

More storage

Installation of convenient places for storing things and hygiene products

Mixers and plumbing control

User-friendly features such as handles with large levers or touch controls

SMART Toilets with Bide

Enhancing hygiene and safety, simplifying daily life for seniors and individuals with disabilities through automation and accessibility

Good lighting and contrast colors

Good lighting and use of contrasting colors to improve visibility and orientation in space
